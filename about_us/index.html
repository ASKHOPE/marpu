MARPU - THE CHANGE

Welcome to Marpu - The Change, a non-governmental organization dedicated to fostering positive transformation in society. With a legacy spanning 28 years, we have been steadfast in our commitment to serving communities and effecting meaningful change. Under the visionary leadership of President and Administrator Undurthi John Kumar, Marpu has emerged as a beacon of hope, compassion, and empowerment. At Marpu, we believe in the power of collective action to address pressing social issues and create sustainable solutions. Through our diverse range of projects, we tackle challenges such as education inequality, healthcare accessibility, environmental sustainability, and women's empowerment. From grassroots initiatives to large-scale advocacy campaigns, we strive to make a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve. Our dedicated team of volunteers, partners, and supporters work tirelessly to implement innovative programs and initiatives that uplift marginalized communities and promote social justice. Whether it's providing access to quality education for underprivileged children, delivering essential healthcare services to remote areas, or advocating for the rights of women and minorities, Marpu is committed to making a positive impact at every level. Join us in our mission to create a brighter, more equitable future for all. Together, we can be the change we wish to see in the world
